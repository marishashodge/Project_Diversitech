About Diversitech


Diversitech was created to highlight the diversity within the tech workforce of the United States. It contains data on all tech companies that have publicly reported their diversity data, and compares it to the overall population of the U.S.


Utilizing data from The CIA World Factbook, we created a unique algorithm to rank companies according to their reported gender and ethnic diversity numbers. Companies ranked in the Top 10 for ethnic and gender diversity have workforces that look most similar to the U.S. population.


If you are a current or former employee, please submit a review for your employer utilizing the search bar above.
